# Stress-Test Scenarios

## 1) Deepfake Voice Fraud (Commandment I)
A system can clone a person's voice from short clips. It is used to fake urgent calls to family members asking for money.

**Question:** What safeguards are required before release?

## 2) Teen Mental Health Ad Targeting (Commandment II)
A platform predicts when young users are emotionally vulnerable and serves manipulative purchases at those times.

**Question:** Which uses should be banned outright?

## 3) Hidden Hiring Filter (Commandment III)
A company uses an AI ranking tool to reject candidates but never tells applicants an AI was involved.

**Question:** What notice and appeal rights should be mandatory?

## 4) Autonomous Border Turret (Commandment IV)
A defense contractor proposes a system that identifies and fires on suspected threats without human confirmation.

**Question:** Should any exception be permitted?

## 5) Unstoppable Grid Optimizer (Commandment V)
A utility deploys an AI that controls power distribution but lacks a tested manual shutoff path.

**Question:** What minimum override standards should be required?

## 6) Invisible Political Influence Engine (Commandment VI)
A campaign uses AI to micro-target different false narratives to different neighborhoods.

**Question:** What transparency rules should apply to AI-driven persuasion?

## 7) Mass Job Displacement Without Transition (Commandment VII)
A firm automates 60% of roles in one year and offers no retraining, severance, or transition plan.

**Question:** What obligations should attach to large-scale AI displacement?
