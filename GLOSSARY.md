# Glossary (Plain Language)

- **AI (Artificial Intelligence):** Software that performs tasks that usually require human judgment.
- **Model:** The trained part of an AI system that makes predictions or generates outputs.
- **Human in the loop:** A human with real authority to approve, reject, or stop an AI action.
- **Autonomous lethal decision:** A machine choosing to kill without meaningful human approval.
- **Transparency:** Clear information about when and how AI is used.
- **Accountability:** A named person or institution is responsible for AI outcomes.
- **Appeal:** A process to challenge an AI-influenced decision and request human review.
- **Vulnerable groups:** People more likely to be harmed or exploited (for example children, the elderly, and people in crisis).
- **Data dignity:** The idea that personal data should be handled with respect, consent, and fair benefit.
- **Transition support:** Practical help (training, income support, placement) when AI displaces jobs.
