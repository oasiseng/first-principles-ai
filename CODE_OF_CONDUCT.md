# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, and evidence-oriented community.

## Expected behavior

- Use clear, constructive language.
- Critique ideas, not people.
- Prioritize impacts on vulnerable populations.
- Provide sources for factual claims.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, or intimidation.
- Bad-faith disruption of review processes.
- Knowingly misleading claims about safety outcomes.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or restrict participation for repeated violations.

Report concerns to project maintainers through repository contact channels.
